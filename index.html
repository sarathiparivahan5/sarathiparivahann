What is Sarathi Parivahan?

Sarathi Parivahan is an online platform that provides digital driving licence services. It helps citizens apply for licences, renew existing licences, update details, and track applications without visiting the RTO frequently.

Services Available on Sarathi Parivahan

🚗 Learner Licence Apply Online

Applicants can apply for a learner licence online by submitting details and completing the required process.

🪪 Driving Licence Application

Users can apply for a permanent driving licence after completing learner licence requirements.

🔄 Driving Licence Renewal

Licence holders can request renewal and update their licence information online.

📌 Driving Licence Status Check

Applicants can track their application progress and check their current licence status online.

How to Check Driving Licence Status Online?

  1. Visit the online driving licence service portal.
  2. Select your state and RTO details.
  3. Enter your application information.
  4. Check your current licence application status.
